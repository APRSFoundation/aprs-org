APRS SKYWARN NETWORK

Aside from GPS tracking, Amateur Radio operators nationwide use APRS for reporting and tracking severe weather conditions. Any station with a home weather station can connect it to his APRS station and automatically transmit his weather conditions. Here is a snapshot of the existing network in the midwest in early 1996.
