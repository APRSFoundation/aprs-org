APRS at the Marine Corps Marathon

Bob Bruninga, WB4APR, US Naval Academy Satellite Lab, Annapolis, MD

This is a reply of APRS at the 1993 Marine Corps Marathon in Washington DC. APRS units were on the lead runner and lead handicapped chase vehicles and tail-end charlie. Also watch for reported positions of downed runners and medical emergencies.

javAPRS Commands (Case Insensitive)
U or D
 zooms up/down (you may also use PGup/dn)
L, S, or M
 List stations, Show Status or Messages to Java console
CTRL or ALT-click
 Centers or Zooms map on clicked location
Arrow keys
 scrolls map
