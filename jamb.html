APRS AT BSA JAMBOREE
23 JULY - 5 AUG 1997

    This APRS map will show the current location of any Ham Radio APRS stations in and around the Jamboree area. To zoom out to see all of Fort A.P. Hill or the East Central US APRS network or the Nationwide HF network, click on the map menu below. For a detailed map of Ft A.P. Hill click here










javAPRS Commands (Case Insensitive)
U or D
 zooms up/down (you may also use PGup/dn)
L, S, or M
 List stations, Show Status or Messages to Java console
CTRL or ALT-click
 Centers or Zooms map on clicked location
Arrow keys
 scrolls map
