Operation On-Target Plans

Bob Bruninga, WB4APR, US Naval Academy, Annapolis, MD

This page will be used as a planning document to see where other scout and youth groups plan on climbing to participate in Operation On Target.

All we need is your LAT/LONG and group name and we will add you to the map. You can zoom in on the map to resolve close together groups. Just send your LAT/LONG to wb4apr@amsat.org
